GALAGACUS V3: ARMADA RISING

Open Galagacus_V3.html in Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari.
No installation is required.

CONTROLS
- Move: Arrow keys or WASD
- Fire: Space or Z
- Nova / Singularity: X, B, or Ctrl
- Focus movement: Shift
- Pause: P
- Sound toggle: M
- Upgrade choice: 1, 2, or 3

V3 FEATURES
- Four selectable ship classes, including a persistent-credit unlock
- Fifteen-wave campaign across five visual sectors
- Named bosses on waves 5, 10, and 15
- Tractor-beam capture and rescue mechanic
- Persistent high score, credits, unlocks, and victories
- Prism volleys, solar lance, hunter rockets, void plasma
- Chain lightning, black-hole nova, drones, shields, and overdrive
- Procedural arcade soundtrack and expanded effects
- Final victory ending
